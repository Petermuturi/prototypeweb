Information System

An Information System is the arrangement of people, data, processes and information that work together to support and improve the day-today operations of a business.

Circumstances of a new Information System

New opportunities a chance to improve the quality of internal processes and service delivery in the organization may arise

Invention of new systems which are more successful than the existing ones

Problems the user may encounter some difficulties in the operations of the existing system

Directive these are requirements imposed by the management, government, or external influences.

Roles of a System Analyst

1: Examines the feasibility of potential computer applications.

2: Reviews the existing system & makes recommendations on how to improve or implement an alternative system.

3: Works hand in hand with programmers to construct a computerized system

4: Coordinates the training of new system users and owners.

5: He is the overall project manager of the information system being implemented. Some of his duties include: assuring quality, keeping within schedule & budgeting.
